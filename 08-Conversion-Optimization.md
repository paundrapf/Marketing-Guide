TAKTIK 1 - LANDING PAGE ABOVE THE FOLD

Must-Have Elements

1. Headline (clear value proposition)
2. Subheadline (expand benefit)
3. Visual (product/result)
4. Primary CTA button
5. Trust element (social proof)

Formula

Above fold = Answer 3 questions dalam 3 detik:
- What is this?
- What's in it for me?
- What do I do next?

Bad Example

Headline: "Welcome to Our Platform"
(Vague, no benefit)

Good Example

Headline: "Get More Done in Less Time"
Subhead: "Our tool handles the busy work. You focus on growth."
Visual: Dashboard screenshot
CTA: "Start Free Trial"
Trust: "Trusted by 10,000+ professionals"

TAKTIK 2 - CTA BUTTON OPTIMIZATION

Color Psychology

Green: "Go", positive action (Convert well)
Orange/Red: Urgency (Use for limited offers)
Blue: Trust (Safe for corporate)

Test: Button color yang kontras dengan page background.

Text Best Practices

Bad: "Submit", "Click Here"
Good: "Get Started Now", "Start Free", "Yes, I Want This"

Button text should:
- Start dengan verb
- Describe benefit
- First-person perspective

Size & Placement

Big enough untuk easy click (min 44x44px mobile).
Place above fold + repeat di bottom.

TAKTIK 3 - PRICING PAGE OPTIMIZATION

Use Anchoring

Show most expensive first, atau strikethrough "original" price.

Decoy Effect

3 tiers where middle highlighted "Most Popular". Majority choose middle.

Annual vs Monthly

Show annual with "Save 20%" badge. Display monthly price dengan "billed annually" small text.

Social Proof per Tier

"Join 5,000 users on Pro tier" - makes it feel safe choice.

FAQ Below Pricing

Overcome objections immediately: "Can I cancel anytime?" "Money-back guarantee?"

TAKTIK 4 - TRUST ELEMENTS Strategy

Types

1. Testimonials (real photo + name + result)
2. Reviews aggregate (4.9/5 from 500 reviews)
3. Trust badges (SSL, payment logos, certifications)
4. Media mentions ("Featured on...")
5. Customer logos (if B2B)
6. Guarantees ("30-day money-back")
7. Live stats ("1,247 users online")

Placement

Sprinkle throughout page. Especially near CTA buttons.

Video Testimonials

3x more credible than text. Even simple phone recording works.

TAKTIK 5 - CHECKOUT FLOW Optimization

Reduce Friction

Steps needed untuk complete purchase:
- Bad: 5+ steps
- Good: 2-3 steps

Show Progress

"Step 1 of 3" progress bar reduces abandonment.

Guest Checkout

Don't force account creation before purchase. Offer after.

Security Reassurance

SSL badge, "Secure checkout", payment logos visible.

TAKTIK 6 - EXIT-INTENT POPUPS

Konsep

Detect ketika user mau leave page, show popup sebagai last chance untuk convert.

What to Offer

1. Discount code ("Wait! Get 10% off")
2. Lead magnet ("Before you go, grab free guide")
3. Free trial extension ("Try 7 more days free")
4. Quiz/assessment ("Not sure? Take this quiz")

Design Best Practice

- Clear headline
- Single CTA
- Easy close button (don't trap users)
- Mobile-friendly (swipe up, not mouse-based)

Tools

OptinMonster, Sumo, Privy, Hello Bar

Impact

5-10% of exiting visitors can convert. That's significant at scale.

TAKTIK 7 - SOCIAL PROOF NOTIFICATIONS

Konsep

Real-time notifications showing recent actions. "John from Jakarta just signed up."

Psychology

FOMO + Validation. "Others doing it = must be good."

Implementation

Tools: Proof, TrustPulse, Fomo.com

Display:
- Recent sign-ups
- Recent purchases
- Current viewers ("47 people viewing this now")
- Recent reviews received

Warning

Must be REAL data. Fake notifications = trust destroyed.

Frequency

1 notification per 30-60 seconds. More = annoying.

TAKTIK 8 - FAQ STRATEGY

Purpose

Pre-empt objections before they become reasons not to buy.

FAQs to Always Include

1. Pricing clarification
2. Refund/cancellation policy
3. Technical requirements
4. Support availability
5. Comparison with competitors
6. Security/privacy concerns

Format

Accordion style (click to expand). Keep page clean.

Placement

Below pricing table. After user sees options, addresses doubts immediately.

Pro Tip

Add FAQ di page yang paling banyak drop-offs. Analyze heatmaps untuk identify where.

TAKTIK 9 - VIDEO ON LANDING PAGE

Impact

Pages with video can increase conversion by 80%. Powerful.

Types of Video

1. Explainer (what & how)
2. Demo (product in action)
3. Testimonial (customer stories)
4. Founder story (personal connection)

Best Practices

- Under 2 minutes
- Autoplay OFF (annoying otherwise)
- Subtitles ON (80% watch muted)
- Thumbnail matters (custom > auto)
- CTA at video end

Placement

Above fold atau right below hero section.

Tools

Loom (quick recording), Wistia/Vimeo (hosting with analytics)

TAKTIK 10 - MOBILE OPTIMIZATION

Reality

70% web traffic dari mobile. Kalau mobile experience jelek, lo lose majority visitors.

Checklist

1. Responsive design (auto-adjust screen size)
2. Touch-friendly buttons (min 44x44px)
3. Fast load (under 3 seconds)
4. Readable text (16px minimum)
5. Easy navigation (thumb-reachable)
6. No horizontal scroll
7. Forms simple (minimal fields)

Testing

- Google Mobile-Friendly Test (free)
- Test on actual devices (not just emulators)
- Check major browsers (Chrome, Safari)

Common Issues

- Popups covering screen
- Images too large (slow load)
- Forms terlalu panjang
- CTA buttons too small

Fix mobile first. Desktop follows.

Rangkuman Bagian 8

Conversion optimization = marginal gains yang compound.

A/B Testing Priority

Test in order:
1. Headline (biggest impact)
2. CTA text dan color
3. Social proof placement
4. Pricing presentation
5. Page length (long vs short)
6. Images/videos
7. Form fields (less = better)

Run test minimum 1 minggu atau 1,000 visitors untuk statistical significance.

Tools Recommended

Analytics: Google Analytics, Hotjar (heatmaps)
A/B Testing: Optimizely, VWO, Google Optimize
Forms: Typeform (higher completion)
Live Chat: Intercom (answer objections real-time)

Mindset

Optimization gak pernah "selesai". Always ada yang bisa di-improve.

Small wins compound. 5% better conversion + 10% better traffic + 8% better retention = 24% better business.
