TAKTIK 1 - TWITTER THREAD FORMULA

Structure yang Work

1. Hook (first tweet) - Bold statement/question
2. Setup (tweets 2-3) - Context/problem
3. Value (tweets 4-8) - Main content/lessons
4. Social proof (tweet 9) - Results/testimonials
5. CTA (tweet 10) - Link/follow

Contoh Thread Hook

 "Gue naikin followers dari 0 ke 10K dalam 90 hari.

Tanpa ads. Tanpa spam. Cuma 8 taktik simple.

Thread:"

Best Practice

- Setiap tweet bisa stand alone
- Avg 200-280 characters per tweet (short lebih di-read)
- Space formatting untuk easy scan
- Reply di tweet pertama dengan "Bookmark this" untuk boost engagement
- Tag relevant people di middle (not first tweet)

TAKTIK 2 - LINKEDIN THOUGHT LEADERSHIP

Format

Personal story + Business lesson + Call to reflection

Contoh

"Tadi pagi meeting sama client yang burnout.

Dia bilang: 'Gue udah kerja 16 jam/hari tapi hasil stagnan.'

Problem bukan effort. Problem adalah sistem.

Lesson: Hard work tanpa smart system = hamster wheel.

System beats hustle every time.

What systems have you built di bisnis lo?"

Winning Elements

- Vulnerable (personal story)
- Educational (business insight)
- Engaging (question di akhir)
- Professional tone (not too casual)

TAKTIK 3 - INSTAGRAM CAROUSEL HACK

10-Slide Formula

Slide 1: Hook + Big promise
Slides 2-9: Tips/steps (one per slide)
Slide 10: CTA + recap

Design Tips

- Dark background + white text (or vice versa)
- Large font (size 60+)
- Minimal text per slide
- Consistent color scheme
- Swipe indicator

Engagement Hack

Post carousel, lalu immediately comment "Slide X my favorite. Which is yours?"

People comment their favorite slide = engagement boost = algorithm loves it.

TAKTIK 4 - TIKTOK VIRAL FORMULA

3-Second Rule

Hook dalam 3 detik pertama atau scroll.

Hook Types

- Visual surprise
- Bold text statement
- Question
- Unexpected sound

Script Structure

0-3 sec: Hook
3-8 sec: Setup problem/context
8-25 sec: Solution/value
25-30 sec: CTA

Trends

Ride trending sounds. Original sound harder to viral kecuali konten super strong.

TAKTIK 6 - FACEBOOK GROUP STRATEGY

Konsep

Groups lebih engage daripada Pages. Community feel lebih kuat, algorithm favor groups.

Setup

1. Buat group dengan nama yang search-friendly
2. Set rules jelas (no spam, add value)
3. Post daily untuk build habit members
4. Feature active members
5. Run weekly events (Q&A, challenges)

Content Mix

40% Educational (tips, tutorials)
30% Discussion (polls, questions)
20% User content (member wins, testimonials)
10% Promotional (subtle, value-first)

Growth Hack

Require answer 3 questions saat join. Collect emails, understand pain points, filter spammers.

TAKTIK 7 - INSTAGRAM STORY ENGAGEMENT

Features yang Work

- Polls (binary choice, easy engage)
- Questions (AMA, collect content ideas)
- Quizzes (fun, educational)
- Slider (rate something)
- Countdown (build hype untuk launch)
- Link sticker (drive traffic)

Daily Story Structure

Story 1: Hook (bold statement atau question)
Story 2-4: Value content
Story 5: CTA (link atau poll)

Timing

Post 3-7 stories per hari. Spread throughout day, not all at once.

Pro Tip

Reply to setiap DM yang masuk dari story. Builds connection, signals algorithm positively.

TAKTIK 8 - TWITTER SPACES

Kapan Pakai

Build deeper connection dengan audience. Audio lebih personal daripada text.

Format yang Work

1. Solo commentary (hot takes, updates)
2. Interview guests (borrow audience)
3. Panel discussion (multiple perspectives)
4. AMA (audience questions)
5. Co-hosting (tag team dengan partner)

Best Practices

- Schedule in advance (gives time for RSVP)
- Promote di feed sebelum live
- Duration 30-60 menit optimal
- Record dan clip highlights
- Follow up dengan thread recap

Audience Building

Invite other hosts as speaker. Their followers see your Space notification.

TAKTIK 9 - LINKEDIN ENGAGEMENT PODS

Konsep

Small group yang commit untuk engage konten masing-masing. Boost awal = algorithm advantage.

Struktur

5-10 orang di niche sama. Agreement untuk like dan comment setiap post dalam 30 menit pertama.

Rules yang Work

1. Max 1 post per orang per hari
2. Genuine comments (bukan spam)
3. Consistent participation
4. Same industry/niche for relevance

Warning

LinkedIn bisa detect artificial engagement. Keep it natural, genuine comments only.

Alternative

Tanpa formal pod, actively engage peers posts secara organic. Reciprocity principle applies.

TAKTIK 10 - INSTAGRAM REELS HOOK

TAKTIK 11 - TWITTER REPLY GUY STRATEGY

Konsep

Engage tweet big accounts untuk borrow their audience. Done right = massive reach.

Rules

1. Add value (insight, question, story) bukan cuma "great post!"
2. Be early (first 30 minutes reply gets most traction)
3. Relevant dan on-topic
4. Consistent (same accounts regularly)
5. Never spammy atau self-promotional

Finding Big Accounts

List 20-30 accounts di niche lo dengan 10K+ followers. Turn on notifications.

Progression

Reply Guy (add value) > They notice you > They engage back > They mention you > Your audience grows.

Time Investment

30-60 menit per hari dedicated untuk quality replies.

TAKTIK 12 - CROSS-PLATFORM REPURPOSING

System

One content, many platforms with format adjustments.

Transformation Matrix

Twitter thread > LinkedIn article
YouTube video > TikTok clips > IG Reels
Podcast > Blog post > Email newsletter
Tweet > Instagram quote graphic
Long video > Shorts/Reels/TikTok

Tools

Descript (video clips), Canva (graphics), Repurpose.io (automation)

Timing

Stagger posts. Gak harus same day. Twitter Monday > LinkedIn Wednesday > IG Friday.

Customization

Tweak format dan tone per platform. LinkedIn more professional, TikTok more casual.

TAKTIK 13 - MEME MARKETING

Konsep

Cultural references dan humor untuk connect dengan audience. High share, high brand recall.

When Works

1. Brand personality allows (not too corporate)
2. Understand target audience culture
3. Timing perfect (trending meme)
4. Execution clean (quality design)

Meme Types

1. Trending format (react image + text)
2. Industry in-jokes
3. Before/after memes
4. Expectation vs reality
5. Starter pack

Risks

Miss the joke = cringe. Old meme = out of touch. Offensive = backfire.

Tools

Canva, Meme Generator, imgflip (templates)

TAKTIK 14 - PLATFORM ALGORITHM HACKS 2024

Twitter/X

- First hour engagement crucial
- Threads outperform single tweets
- Images and polls boost reach
- Replies to your own tweets = chain engagement
- Blue check = algorithm boost

Instagram

- Reels over static images
- Save and share metrics outweigh likes
- DM conversations boost relationship signals
- Carousel = longer time on post = favor
- First 30 min engagement matters

LinkedIn

- Dwell time (how long people read) metrics
- Comments outweigh reactions
- Post timing: Tuesday-Thursday 8-10am
- Document posts (carousels) perform well
- Personal stories outperform corporate

TikTok

- Watch time percentage = king metric
- Loop videos (seamless repeat)
- Reply to comments with video
- Trending sounds mandatory
- Post 2-3x daily for algorithm training

TAKTIK 15 - HASHTAG STRATEGY

Twitter

2-3 hashtags max. Lebih = spammy.
Mix: 1 broad (#marketing) + 1 niche (#[yourindustry]) + 1 branded (#[yourbrand])

Instagram

10-15 hashtags. Mix sizes:
- 5 small (under 100K posts)
- 5 medium (100K-500K)
- 5 large (500K+)

Place di caption atau first comment (both work).

LinkedIn

3-5 hashtags. Professional focus.
Include industry hashtag always.

TikTok

3-5 hashtags. Trending + niche mix.
Use "For You Page" markers (#fyp, #foryou) sparingly.

Research Tools

Display Purposes (Instagram), Hashtagify (Twitter), TikTok search (native)

Rangkuman Bagian 5

15 taktik social media bukan untuk dipake semua. Focus 1-2 platform, master dulu, baru expand.

Platform Comparison

Twitter: Real-time, threads, text-heavy
LinkedIn: Professional, long-form, thought leadership
Instagram: Visual, stories, reels
TikTok: Short video, trends, entertainment
YouTube: Long-form video, SEO, evergreen

Pick primary platform sesuai audience lo. Repurpose ke sisanya.
