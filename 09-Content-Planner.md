BAGIAN 1
CONTENT CALENDAR MINGGUAN

Template Twitter/X

Senin: Educational Thread
- Topic: How-to atau framework
- Format: 8-10 tweet thread
- Goal: Establish expertise

Selasa: Personal Story
- Topic: Behind-the-scenes atau journey
- Format: Single tweet atau short thread
- Goal: Build connection

Rabu: Hot Take / Controversial Opinion
- Topic: Industry opinion yang polarizing
- Format: Single tweet, invite debate
- Goal: Engagement + reach

Kamis: User Content / Testimonial
- Topic: Feature customer win atau results
- Format: Screenshot + quote tweet
- Goal: Social proof

Jumat: Case Study / Results
- Topic: Detailed breakdown of success
- Format: Thread dengan numbers
- Goal: Authority + conversion

Sabtu: Entertainment / Meme
- Topic: Light content, industry humor
- Format: Image atau short video
- Goal: Reach + likes

Minggu: Engagement Post
- Topic: Ask question atau poll
- Format: Single tweet dengan question
- Goal: Replies + algorithm boost

Weekly Review Template

Every Sunday spend 30 menit:

1. Top 3 performing posts this week
- Why did they work?
- What can I replicate?

2. Bottom 3 performing posts
- Why didn't they work?
- What to avoid?

3. Audience engagement patterns
- Best posting times?
- Which topics resonate?

4. Competitor observation
- What are they posting?
- What's working for them?

5. Next week adjustments
- What to do more?
- What to stop?
- What to test?

BAGIAN 5
CONTENT REPURPOSING SYSTEM

One Piece, Many Platforms

Start with: 1 long-form content (thread, blog, video)

Repurpose to:
1. Twitter thread > Break into single tweets (5-7 tweets)
2. Thread > LinkedIn article (expand formal tone)
3. Thread > Instagram carousel (10 slides)
4. Thread > TikTok/Reels script (30-60 sec)
5. Thread > Newsletter email
6. Thread > Quote graphics (3-5 images)
7. Thread > Podcast talking points

Transformation Timeline

Day 1: Original Twitter thread
Day 2: Instagram carousel version
Day 3: LinkedIn expanded post
Day 4: TikTok/Reel video
Day 5: Email newsletter
Day 6-7: Single tweet reminders

Maximum reach from minimum effort.

BAGIAN 6
CONTENT BATCHING

System untuk Efficiency

Hari Khusus:
- 1 hari planning (outline all content for week)
- 1 hari creation (write all drafts)
- 1 hari editing dan scheduling
- Sisa hari: engagement dan monitoring

Batching Session Structure

Planning Session (2 hours):
- Review last week performance
- List content topics for week
- Research trending topics
- Outline thread structures

Creation Session (4 hours):
- Write all threads/posts
- Create visuals
- Record videos
- Draft captions

Scheduling Session (1 hour):
- Load content ke scheduler
- Set posting times
- Prepare engagement reminders
- Final review

Tools untuk Batching

Scheduling: Buffer, Hootsuite, Later, TweetHunter
Writing: Notion, Google Docs
Design: Canva, Figma
Video: CapCut, Descript

BAGIAN 7
CONTENT PILLAR TEMPLATE

3-5 Content Pillars untuk Focus

Contoh untuk Digital Product (Course/Template/SaaS):

Pillar 1: Core Topic (Your Expertise)
- How-to guides
- Frameworks dan strategies
- Tips dan best practices

Pillar 2: Productivity / Transformation
- Time-saving methods
- Efficiency hacks
- Before/after results

Pillar 3: Behind-the-Scenes
- Product updates
- Building journey
- Lessons learned

Pillar 4: Customer Success
- Testimonials
- Case studies
- User wins

Pillar 5: Industry / Hot Takes
- Trends dan news
- Controversial opinions
- Engagement discussions

Content Mix

30% Pillar 1 (main expertise)
25% Pillar 2 (transformation)
20% Pillar 3 (personal brand)
15% Pillar 4 (social proof)
10% Pillar 5 (engagement bait)
